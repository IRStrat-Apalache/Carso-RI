Environmental

At Fibra Educa, we are committed to optimizing the efficient use of resources in order to minimize the environmental impact of our tenants' operations. Through initiatives aligned with our sustainability strategy, we encourage more responsible and environmentally friendly consumption.

We focus on improving the operating efficiency of our properties to achieve certification as green buildings and on the transition from fossil energy to renewable sources.

Our goal is to certify 30% of our gross rentable area as sustainable and to migrate 45% of our energy consumption to renewable energy, with a baseline year of 2022.

Energy

At Fibra Educa, we are continuously working to optimize our energy consumption and that of our tenants, implementing measures that improve energy efficiency. We monitor our consumption through electricity bills, with data from 100% of our tenants.

We have set ourselves the goal that, by 2030, 45% of Fibra Educa's energy consumption will come from renewable sources. To achieve this goal, we are installing photovoltaic panels in our buildings, taking as a reference the energy consumption of 2022, which was 3,198,967 kWh.

Emissions
