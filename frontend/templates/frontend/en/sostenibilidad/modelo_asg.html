Materiality and Sustainable Strategy

Sustainability is one of the central axes of our business model. We are aware that the decisions we make today impact the future, affecting not only us, but also today's society and future generations.

As the only Fibra in the country focused on educational buildings, it understands that its strategy allows thousands of young people to study in quality infrastructure, training them as leaders of tomorrow. This, in turn, contributes to Mexico's economic growth and development, improving governance, closing inequality gaps, and promoting equity.

Our materiality matrix identifies the most important issues for both our business and our stakeholders. This analysis allowed us to define 12 priority issues on which we focus our efforts, thus ensuring sustainable growth that meets the expectations of all our stakeholders.

Our sustainability strategy is based on the 12 material themes we identify in our materiality matrix. These issues are key to ensuring that our operations are responsible and aligned with the expectations of our stakeholders. SDG contribution

At Fibra Educa, we reaffirm our commitment with the 2030 Agenda and the Sustainable Development Goals. To maximize the impact of our initiatives, and in accordance with our strategy, we identified the SDGs most influenced by our operations, and the goals to which we contribute through the management of our material issues. In this regard, our actions focus on 11 specific SDGs, as shown below:

Facilitating access to education for the most vulnerable sectors. Through its scholarship program, it generates a positive impact by promoting social mobility, ensuring that people living in poverty have equal access to economic resources and basic services, such as education, which strengthens their opportunities for development and well-being.

Our employees are trained according to the needs of the position they occupy to ensure their professional development.

We offer equal opportunities in all our work processes, from hiring to development, growth and compensation, avoiding any type of discrimination.

We implemented several energy eco-efficiency initiatives, such as LED lighting and renewable energy management in our buildings.

We developed and implemented a solid governance structure, complying with the best corporate governance practices.

Committed to transparency, we publish our annual sustainability report 2022, in which we showcase our ESG performance.
